Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 8, 2024

Thank you for using Rise Up. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our habit tracker app.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information from our users. All data you enter into the app (e.g., habits, goals, and reminders) is stored locally on your device.

Use of Data

Your data is used solely for the purpose of tracking your habits and progress. We do not share, sell, or use your data for any other purpose.

Third-Party Services

The app may display advertisements through third-party ad providers. These services may collect data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

In-App Purchases

Rise Up offers an option to remove ads through a one-time in-app purchase. No personal data is required for this transaction, and payments are processed securely via the app store.

Your Privacy Rights

You have full control over your data. You can clear or delete your data anytime by uninstalling the app.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be effective upon posting the updated policy in the app.
